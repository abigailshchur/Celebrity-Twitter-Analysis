How did the top Twitter users tweet about various topics in the past two years?

How did the top Twitter users tweet @ each other in the past two years?

Here we examine how the top Twitter users interact with one another. For each user, we searched for all instances of @(other user) and recorded said tweets. Then, we ran those tweets through a sentiment analysis. For each interaction, we placed the Tweeter on the left axis and the Tweetee on the top and bottom axes.
